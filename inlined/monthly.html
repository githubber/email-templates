This month in Sunlight

Welcome to the inaugural issue of This Month in Sunlight, a new offering that will provide you with updates on what we’re up to at the Sunlight Foundation. Expect a single digest to arrive on the first week of each month, featuring a few great stories and a surprise or two (GIFs? Photos of our analog coffee clock?). Stay tuned!
